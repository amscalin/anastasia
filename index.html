The Method

Four principles that change everything about how a stepper works.

A stepper is one of the most underused pieces of equipment in any home — because the people using it have never been taught what it's actually for.

01 / Activation

Glutes, not quadriceps

The way most people step builds bulky thighs. The cues in this program reroute that force into the glutes and hamstrings — where you actually want it.

02 / Alignment

Knees, hips, spine

Eliminate the joint pain that comes from poor stepper form. Every drill is grounded in alignment principles drawn from Pilates and functional movement.

03 / Breath

Thoracic mobility

Open the upper back so the diaphragm and glutes can work together. This is the unlock most programs ignore entirely.

04 / Progression

Three structured levels

Foundation, Intensity, Sculpt. You begin where your body actually is, not where you wish it were — and progress when your form earns it.

05 / Diagnostic

Personal video review

In the full tier, Anastasia reviews a short clip of how you move and returns a custom audio note — corrections specific to your body.

06 / Recovery

Pelvic floor work

Dedicated sessions for the deep core and pelvic floor — the foundation that makes every other muscle in your body work better.
